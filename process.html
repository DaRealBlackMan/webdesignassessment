This is how the helmet started out as. It was originally the default square mesh, but I used the subdivide smooth option and made it into three cuts to form its spherical-like shape.


Then I cut the subdivided mesh in half and started to make the shape of a helmet.


I selected some faces and extended them down to make the ear muffs/pads and neck pads which formed the basic shape of a helmet.


Once I finished making the basic helmet shape I made the ear pads stick out more to look more detailed and realistic as some helmets have ear muffs to cushion/protect the ear and to comfort it.


After editing the shape of the helmet and giving it a new, smooth texture I coloured it black and made a design with a red line on top running down the middle of the helmet to give it a nice appealing look.
